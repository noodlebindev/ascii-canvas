## Why this works

- Seven events fit comfortably in a vertical timeline at 100 cols — the widest line is the description text at roughly 70 chars, well inside budget.
- Two-line descriptions (indented below the title) give richer context than the format's skeleton one-liner, which is the "richer than the inline example" requirement for this artifact class.
- The final event drops the `│` connector to signal it is the last milestone, matching the canonical skeleton's convention for the terminal event.
